Grama Panchayat Services

Birth Certificate

Official record of a child's birth issued by the Panchayat.
Documents Required:
  • Hospital Birth Record
  • Parent Aadhaar Copies
  • Application Form

Death Certificate

Legal proof of death required for official purposes.
Documents Required:
  • Hospital Death Record
  • Applicant Aadhaar
  • Application Form

Marriage Certificate

Official registration of marriage under Panchayat records.
Documents Required:
  • Marriage Proof
  • Bride & Groom Aadhaar
  • Passport Photos

Income Certificate

Certificate showing annual income for scholarships and benefits.
Documents Required:
  • Income Proof
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Ration Card Copy

Building Permit

Approval required before constructing or modifying buildings.
Documents Required:
  • Building Plan
  • Land Ownership Document
  • Tax Receipt

Trade License

Permission to legally operate business within Panchayat limits.
Documents Required:
  • Business Registration Proof
  • Owner Aadhaar
  • Location Proof
